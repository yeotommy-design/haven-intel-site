Policy

Responsible Use

HavenIntel is designed for adults who want calm football analysis, structured match reading, and public post-match accountability. It should be used responsibly and with clear expectations about uncertainty.

Adult audience only

HavenIntel is not intended for minors. The service, its editorial framing, and its private member products are written for adults and should not be promoted or presented as youth-oriented content.

Use analysis as analysis

HavenIntel publishes football analysis, not certainty. Readers should approach every match brief, archive review, and score-range note as structured interpretation rather than a guaranteed path.

Avoid compulsive behavior

If football-outcome content begins to create pressure, compulsive checking, or unhealthy decision-making, the responsible step is to pause and disengage. HavenIntel is designed to be measured, not obsessive.

No chase language

HavenIntel does not position missed reads as reasons to double down, chase, or seek recovery. The archive and review system exists to explain what held and what missed, not to intensify reaction.

Respect local rules

Visitors and members are responsible for ensuring that their use of football analysis content is lawful in their own location and appropriate to their own circumstances.
