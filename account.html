Privacy reassurance

HavenIntel uses your member email to manage account access, delivery history, and the release window for private match briefs. Public pages do not expose your private package access.

What the account unlocks

  • 1. Package access saved to one member profile.
  • 2. Release-window delivery to your email address.
  • 3. Account dashboard with upcoming brief status.
  • 4. Private match brief visibility before full time.

Member rules

  • 1. User creates account with valid email.
  • 2. User unlocks a HavenIntel package from the access page.
  • 3. Access is saved to the member profile.
  • 4. Match brief is sent to the member email address.
  • 5. Match brief is released 6 hours or less before kickoff.
