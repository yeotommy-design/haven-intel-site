HavenIntel is still in public soft launch. This page explains the current support status and what will be in place before paid access opens.

Current support status

Public pages are live, but backend user email and paid-member delivery are not active yet. That means HavenIntel is not currently operating a full live customer-support workflow for paid access.

What will change before paid launch

Before paid access opens, HavenIntel will publish a clear working support channel, contact response expectations, and the operational email path tied to account and delivery issues.

What this means for visitors today

Visitors can review the public site, the archive, the sample brief, and the policy stack now. However, no one should assume active payment support, member inbox support, or automated release delivery until those systems are explicitly announced as live.

Operational honesty

HavenIntel would rather state its support limitations clearly during soft launch than imply that a full paid-service operation already exists. These trust pages are part of that clarity.
