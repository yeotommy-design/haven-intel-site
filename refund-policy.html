Refund Policy

Digital access products

This page explains HavenIntel’s refund position for digital football analysis releases, member access, and archived access products.

General rule

HavenIntel provides digital analysis products and access-based releases. Once a purchase is completed, it is generally treated as final because the product is digital, time-sensitive, and tied to a release window rather than a physical shipment.

No guaranteed outcomes

Refund requests will not be accepted on the basis that a match result, scoreline, or football outcome did not unfold as expected. HavenIntel provides analysis and match tracking, not guaranteed results.

Non-delivery review

If a purchased release is not delivered at all because of a clear internal service failure, HavenIntel may review the case manually. Any goodwill response remains discretionary unless a different outcome is required by applicable law.

Member responsibility

Members are responsible for entering a valid email address, reviewing package details before purchase, and understanding the release window attached to time-sensitive football analysis products.

Legal exceptions

Nothing on this page limits any refund or consumer right that must be provided under applicable law. Where the law requires a different treatment, HavenIntel will follow that requirement.
