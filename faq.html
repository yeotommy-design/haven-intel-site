FAQ

Quick answers for visitors who want to understand what HavenIntel is, what is already live, and what is still being built before paid access opens.

Is HavenIntel live?

Yes, the public site is live. The board, archive, insights pages, and trust pages are visible now as part of soft launch.

Can I buy access yet?

Not yet. Pricing and product structure are visible, but payments are not active and paid access has not opened.

Are member emails already working?

No. Backend email delivery for users is not yet active, which is one reason HavenIntel remains in soft-launch mode rather than a live paid-delivery state.

Why show a sample brief publicly?

Because visitors should be able to see the product texture before any paid path opens. The public sample shows the structure, tone, and accountability layer in one place.

What is the archive for?

The archive is where completed matches are reviewed in public. It exists so visitors can see what held, what missed, and how the brief compares with the final score.
