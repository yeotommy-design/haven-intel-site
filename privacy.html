Policy

Privacy

This page explains, at a high level, what HavenIntel may collect from members and visitors, how that information is used, and the limited purpose behind that collection.

Information collected

HavenIntel may collect basic account details such as name, email address, and package-access history. Additional operational data, including release status, account activity, and limited payment-related references, may also be stored where needed to manage access and delivery.

How information is used

Member data is used to manage account access, send football insights releases, maintain delivery records, and support the internal operation of the service. HavenIntel does not position collected data as a marketing asset first; it is primarily used to operate the service itself.

Communications

When a member purchases access, HavenIntel may send account-related emails, release-window emails, and service notices tied to match coverage, package access, or operational updates.

Third-party services

Payment processors, email services, hosting tools, and analytics or delivery tools may handle limited data as part of running HavenIntel. Where such services are used, they are used only as necessary to operate the product workflow.

Policy updates

HavenIntel may revise this privacy page from time to time as the service evolves, especially when new account, payment, or email-delivery systems are introduced.
